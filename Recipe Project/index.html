Protein Pancakes

If you enjoy working out, it is important that you get your protein in. Protein pancakes
are an easy and delicious way to eat some protein and taste just like any other pancake,
but better!


This homemade recipe is made with the use of Trader Joe'sTM protein pancake mix. This recipe is
the most delicous way of preparing protein pancakes. You can add chocolate chips or fruit of your
choice to add extra flavor to your pancakes.

This is My Homemade Protein Pancake Recipe

  • 3 Parts Trader Joe'sTM Protein Pancake Mix
  • 2 Parts Water
  • 1 Parts Steel Cut Oats
  • Blueberries or Banana
  • Chocolate Chips If You Like Chocolate

Now that you have made your
protein pancakes, eat up!

What to do after eating your protein pancakes

  1. Gather Your Things For the Gym
  2. Bike to the Gym
  3. Warmup
  4. Enjoy Your Workout
  5. Go Home and Rest
